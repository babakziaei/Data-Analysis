PMP Certification Overview

The Project Management Professional (PMP) certification is a globally respected standard that plays a key role in the success of projects at major companies, including IBM and Cisco. These organizations, among many others, rely on PMP-certified professionals to lead their projects, reflecting the certification's widespread recognition and trust.

The PMP exam covers a comprehensive range of knowledge areas and processes, which are detailed in the table below. This certification is uniquely designed to align with both agile and waterfall project management methodologies, making it suitable for any project, regardless of size or industry.
