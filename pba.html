PMI-PBA Certification Overview

The PMI Professional in Business Analysis (PMI-PBA) certification is a globally respected standard that signifies expertise in business analysis. Major companies, such as Deloitte and Accenture, rely on PMI-PBA certified professionals to guide their projects and optimize business processes, emphasizing the certification's high level of recognition and trust across industries.

The PMI-PBA exam covers an extensive range of knowledge areas and processes, which are detailed in the table below. This certification is designed to validate the expertise of professionals who work with stakeholders in defining requirements and shaping the output of projects, ensuring a focused and efficient delivery of solutions.
